Friday Five: 417's Best Events 5/15-5/21

Share

Rain or Shine

Rain or shine we have this weekend and next week's best activities for you! You can find a healthy mixture of indoor and outdoor things to get involved in! Don't miss out on the opportunity to connect with the outdoors or friends.

What's Happening This Week?

Outdoor Dining and Sidewalk Shopping on C-Street

Where: Historic C- Street, 299 E Commercial St, Springfield, MO

When: Saturday, May 16th @ 10 AM - 7 PM

"Welcome Back to C-Street!
Come enjoy all the things you love about Historic Commercial Street.
Many restaurants are open for indoor dining, as well as, outdoor seating on patios (or out front). Several stores will also be open and/or offering sidewalk shopping.."

Taco Tuesday Sunset Paddle

Where: Finley River Park

When: Tuesday, May 19th from 6 PM - 8 PM

"Gravel Bar Tacos and Sunsets. We will provide all the Taco Fixin's while we enjoy them on the beautiful Finley River.

$20.00 - board, paddle, PFD. We would ask for a small donation for food cost be not obligated to do so."

Click here to get your tickets!

Bike, Walk & Wheel Week 2020

Where: Ozark Greenways, Inc.

When: Friday-Sunday May 15-17th from 5 AM - 8 PM

"While many of our normal daily activities may be restricted, being active can invigorate us and boost our optimism. Cycling, walking, and wheeling in our beautiful outdoors all offer a healthy break, both physically and mentally, to those stuck indoors more than usual.

We hope that you will celebrate being active with a bicycle, walking, or wheeling activity. Whether a walk around the block to check on a neighbor or a bicycle ride on a new trail, we hope that you will join the fun.

Additionally, we are highlighting several activities that are happening in our region that will help you get outside and be active.

Be sure to post a pic/selfie on Facebook/Instagram (make sure it is public) and use #BikeWalkWheelWeekSGF to be entered into daily prize drawings and the grand prize drawing on June 1, 2020. "

Pinecone Flower Wreath

Where: Freakin' Antiquin' Vintage Market

When: Sunday, May 17th @ 1:30 PM - 4:30 PM

"NEW CLASS! Second class due to overwhelming response! Come make this fun wreath from pinecones! All material and instruction provided. Sign up in market $30.00 or use the online link."

Click here to sign up!

Sunny Bouquet

Where: Painting with a Twist, 3444 S Campbell Ave, Suite G, Springfield,

When: Saturday, May 16th from 2 PM - 4 PM

"Join us to pain Sunny Bouquet! Limited occupancy to allow for social distancing."

Click here to sign up!
