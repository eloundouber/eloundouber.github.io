Ashley Feliu Rivera

For our spicy Puerto Rican, Christmas is the best time of year. Ashley is an expert in all things Hallmark Christmas Movies and has watched all the new ones for the past 3 years with her trusted fluffy ball of fun- Ollie (an adorable little Pomeranian). One thing we have learned from Ashley is, Puerto Ricans know how to celebrate, and she specializes in bringing her authentic drinks to the TDP family ... Más coquito por favor y vamos de fiesta!

More About Ashley

Ashley is originally from Puerto Rico and moved to Missouri after earning her Bachelor of Architecture with a minor in Historic Preservation degree from the Pontifical Catholic University of Puerto Rico. She studied abroad in Europe where she researched historic architecture in Spain, Italy, France, and Amsterdam.

She also participated in the National Collegiate Entrepreneurs’ Organization Conference, which encourages entrepreneurship in college students. She joins the team as an architectural associate, working towards her licensure and is an associate member of the American Institute of Architects. At TDP, she works on a large variety of projects and stages of design, from early conceptual design research to construction documents and enjoys the client interaction and relationship.

She likes to be involved in the community!
