Griffin Bobbett

Like a dog with a bone, Griffin goes after every project whole-heartedly. Whether it’s working on the farm, coordinating a development project, or playing a pick-up game of basketball, he will tackle it with a zeal all his own. He loves a challenge and wants to give his clients solutions that wow them. Hardly ever sitting still, Griffin prefers to meet people face to face, sometimes in a coffee shop, sometimes over a game of poker (hide your quarters). If it were possible to manage all his projects from a tree stand or in front of the fire at the cabin, he’d try it.

More About Griffin

Griffin is a life long Ozark resident and has developed a passion for helping those around him succeed. Griffin has spent his entire career focused on building wholesome, meaningful relationships. He strives to treat client relationships the same as he would treat his family and friends and prides himself on the relationships he has built and the partnerships he continues to maintain. His experience, hard work, and knowledge have led him to become a partner here at TDP. Griffin is excited to share and be the lead in the new real estate and development services. He has helped develop a new menu of services to help our clients from start to finish and everything in between. If you want to do more than just sell land, Griffin is your guy!
